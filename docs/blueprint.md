# **App Name**: Faslon Ki Rahnumai

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display available insecticides, fungicides, herbicides, and plant growth/nutrition products.
- Category Filters: Allow filtering products by category (Insecticides, Fungicides, Herbicides, Plant Growth) and crop (Cotton, Wheat, Rice, Corn, Vegetables).
- Contact Buttons: Provide 'Call Now' and 'WhatsApp' buttons for easy communication.
- Product Information: Detail original packing, company information, and usage guidance for each product, powered by generative AI tool to generate descriptions.
- Farmers Feedback: Showcase farmers feedback with names and areas to build trust.
- Dosage Calculator: A generative AI tool that generates the proper dosage for each product based on crop, area and conditions entered by the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant green (#32CD32) to represent growth, nature, and agriculture.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F8FF) to provide a clean and fresh backdrop.
- Accent color: Light brown (#BDB76B) to evoke earthiness and trustworthiness.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern and readable appearance. 
- Simple and mobile-first design with a floating WhatsApp button for accessibility.
- Use real product photos of the medicines on the shelf, along with recognizable icons for categories and crops.
- Minimal animations such as smooth transitions to avoid overwhelming the user.
